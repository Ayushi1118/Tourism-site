Kalpeshwar Temple

2,200m · Panch Kedar · Open Throughout the Year

History & Mythology

Kalpeshwar is the last of the **Panch Kedar** temples. It is unique because it remains open year-round when other high-altitude temples are closed due to snow. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva's matted hair (Jata). Legend says Indra gained back his lost kingdom after performing penance to Shiva here.

How to Travel There

  • Base: Urgam Valley, Chamoli district.
  • Access: A short 1-2 km easy walk from the Urgam road-head.
  • Rail: Rishikesh is the nearest major railway station (~250 km).

Quick Facts

Deity: Shiva (Jata)

Altitude: 2,200 m

Open: 365 Days

Winter Places Nearby

  • Urgam Village: A scenic valley famous for its terraces and local agriculture.
  • Dhyan Badri: One of the Sapt Badri temples located in the same valley.
