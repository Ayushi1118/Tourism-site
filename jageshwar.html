Jageshwar Dham

1,870m · Ancient Temple Complex · Almora

History & Heritage

Jageshwar is a magnificent complex of over 100 ancient stone temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Nestled within a dense forest of deodar cedars, the temples represent the **Maru-Gurjara** and Nagara architectural styles. The site is considered one of the holiest places in Kumaon and is believed to have been built by the Katyuri and Chand kings over a thousand years ago.

How to Travel There

  • Location: Almora district, Uttarakhand.
  • Road: Approximately 36 km from Almora town.
  • Rail: Kathgodam (125 km) is the nearest major railway station.

Quick Facts

Deity: Shiva (Lakulesh)

Site: 100+ Ancient Shrines

Environment: Deodar Forest

Winter Places Nearby

  • Vridh Jageshwar: An ancient site offering panoramic Himalayan views nearby.
  • Almora: The cultural capital of Kumaon with historic bazaars.
